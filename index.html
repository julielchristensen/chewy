Our Vision

With Chewy gum our goal is to create a way to support young upcoming artist by letting them design our packaging for our flavourful gum. We strive for making the world a better place and therefor our gum is without plastic, vegan & biogradable!

Past features

We have collaborated with a bunch of fantastic artists and still want to exploit their name.
Here you are gonna find the 3 most recent collaborations we have done!

Mark & Vincent

This duo have known eachother since 5th grade and have always shared a passion of drawing. In 2018 they decided to work together on a artpiece and ever since that, they have comitted to working together as a artistic duo. They make abstract drawings of nature and people, and we loved working together with Mark & Vincent because of their joy and inspiring gait.

Ida Art

Ida Cecilie, also known under her artist name Ida Art, is a young feminist and human-lover. She creates art that challenges social norms and creates debate. It was such a pleasure working with Ida, not only because of her amazing art but because of her view on life and inspiring way of working.

Malthe Ejer

Malthe Ejer is a grafitti artist located in Copenhagen. He has loved drawing and painting since he was a kid. The passion he has is undeniable, and what he creates out of sheer improvisation is very admirable. We had a blast working with Malthe because of his talent and always great mood.
